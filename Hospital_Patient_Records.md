## Massachusetts General Hospital — Performance Dashboard

**Tools used:** Power BI | Power Query | DAX | Data Modelling

**Description:**  
A 3-page Power BI dashboard developed as part of the Maven Analytics 
Hospital Challenge, focused on performance optimisation at Massachusetts 
General Hospital. The dashboard delivers executive-level visibility across 
three key areas: an Executive Summary with headline KPIs, a Financial 
Overview covering revenue trends and insurance claim analysis, and an 
Operational Efficiency page tracking hospital workflow and resource metrics.
