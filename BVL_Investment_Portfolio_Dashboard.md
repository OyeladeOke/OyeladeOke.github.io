## Bond Vision Limited Investment Portfolio Dashboard

**Tools used:** Power BI | Power Query | DAX

**Description:**  
An investment club portfolio tracker built for Bond Vision Limited. 
Tracks Asset Under Management (AUM), Net Asset Value (NAV per unit), Fund ROI, and Members-level P&L across 
Equities, Money Market Funds (MMF), and Fixed Income (FI) instruments.
