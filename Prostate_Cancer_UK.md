## Exploring Ethnic Inequalities in Prostate Cancer Mortality in the UK

**Tools used:** R | PowerPoint | ONS Data

**Description:**  
Prostate cancer is the most common male cancer in the UK, yet outcomes 
vary significantly across ethnic groups. Using ONS data and statistical 
analysis in R, this project investigates how mortality rates differ across 
ethnicities. Key findings reveal that Black Caribbean and Black African men 
face disproportionately higher mortality rates relative to their population 
share — highlighting the urgent need for targeted awareness, early screening 
programmes, and ethnicity-sensitive healthcare policy.
